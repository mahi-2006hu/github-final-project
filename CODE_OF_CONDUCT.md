# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and considerate.
- Communicate openly and thoughtfully.
- Follow project rules and guidelines.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking action when necessary.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project maintainers.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Consequences for unacceptable behavior may range from warnings to banning from the community.

## Attribution
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
